About Us

At SkyReach, we're pioneering a smarter way to deliver essential skincare products using autonomous drone technology. Our mission is to make same-day delivery of lightweight items fast, reliable, and eco-friendly—starting with a curated selection of high-demand skincare essentials.

SkyReach is an early-stage drone delivery startup focused on items under 1 lb, with an initial catalog of 37 skincare products available for delivery in the San Francisco area. We've developed a custom preorder system using Google Apps Script, featuring an image-enhanced selection experience. Our operations are powered by the DJI Mavic 3 Enterprise, enhanced with a cellular dongle for real-time communication and autonomous flight capabilities.

Looking ahead, we're planning to expand our delivery capabilities to support heavier payloads, incorporate AI agents for logistics automation and customer support, and continue refining the overall experience for speed, transparency, and ease of use.

SkyReach is more than delivery—it's the future of convenience, powered by innovation.
